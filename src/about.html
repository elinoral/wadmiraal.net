About

I'm a dutch guy, married to a beautiful french woman and living in Switzerland, in the old and gorgeous city of Fribourg, which also has the particularity of having two official languages (french and german), despite being a city of only ~30'000.

Talk about a cultural mixup...

Anyhow...

I'm a developer for Connect-i, a small but great web development company.

I have the privilege of working on several great and challenging products and have, so far, had the luck of working on both front-end and the back-end code.

Working for Connect-i, I do a lot with Drupal. We attend Drupalcons, sponsor it from time to time and I maintain several Drupal modules, on behalf of Connect-i.

I love my job. Developing has become a passion and I enjoy (almost) every day I spend on the job.

Except coding and crafting intuitive UIs, I thoroughly enjoy reading, which even grew into a little pet project called 100 Must Read Books.

Other than that, I like the classic, boring, I've-read-that-before-stuff, like movies, music, spending time with my family and friends, etc.

I'm mainly a PHP developer, though I try out many different languages and try to keep up with the rapidly changing landscape. At the time of writing, I'm digging into Haskell.

Other than PHP, a large portion of my time goes into Javascript code, HTML markup and CSS (well, SCSS actually) styles.
